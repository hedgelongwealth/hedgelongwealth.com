The Strategy

The Options Wheel, Explained Simply

No guessing. No charts to stare at all day. One repeatable process on stocks we'd happily own.

STEP 1

Sell Cash Secured Puts

Get paid upfront to agree to buy a large-cap stock at a price we already want to own it at. If it doesn't drop there, we keep the income and repeat.

STEP 2

Get Assigned (Sometimes)

If the stock dips to our price, we buy it — at a discount, with the premium already in our pocket.

STEP 3

Sell Covered Calls

Now holding the stock, we sell calls against it for more weekly income, until it's sold back at a profit — then the wheel turns again.
